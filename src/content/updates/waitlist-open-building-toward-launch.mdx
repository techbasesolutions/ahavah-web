Ahavah is being built for a specific purpose: to help Messianic, Hebrew Roots, and Torah-observant believers find a spouse, across borders and across more than 100 languages. Today the waitlist is open, and we want to be clear about where things stand.

## What is ready now

The waitlist is live, and joining takes about a minute. Founding members will be the first invited when matching opens. We have also published a small library of [resources](/resources) on finding a spouse, biblical marriage, and meeting safely across borders.

## What founding members can expect

When invites open, members on the waitlist will receive an email with a one-tap sign-in link. Identity verification is built in, with tiers of trust, so the community starts on an honest footing. Shared-belief filters come first, so you are matching on conviction, not just photos.

## How to be first in line

The single best thing you can do today is [join the waitlist](/waitlist). That is how invites are sent, and that is how you will hear the moment founding-member access opens.

We will keep posting honest updates here as we go.
